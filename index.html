Description

A great way to pickle jalapenos that gives them a nice sweet flavor to balance out the hot.
The recipe can be tinkered with to create more sweet than hot or more hot than sweet depending on the taste desired.

Pickled Peppers

Ingredients

  • 3/4 lb jalapenos
  • 2/3 cup vinegar
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1 mason jar
